Raw Material Rich Biodiversity – Detailed

The park is situated in the transition zone of the Deccan Plateau and Western Ghats, ensuring year‑round availability of:

  • 🍅 Tomato (3 seasons) – 500,000 MT/year
  • 🥭 Mango (Alphonso, Kesar) – 200,000 MT
  • 🍊 Orange & Mosambi – 150,000 MT
  • 🌽 Grains (jowar, bajra, wheat) – 1M MT
  • 🥜 Oilseeds (groundnut, soybean) – 250,000 MT
  • 🥛 Milk (cooperative network) – 1.2M L/day
  • 🍓 Pomegranate, banana, grapes – 500,000 MT
  • 🌶️ Spices (turmeric, ginger, chilli) – 50,000 MT

Over 15,000 local farming families are directly associated with BVG's contract farming programs, ensuring traceability and quality.

BVG Group – Promoter Strength

BVG India Ltd. – India's largest integrated facility management company with 70,000+ employees, serving 750+ clients across 22 states. Annual turnover exceeds ₹2,500 Cr. The group’s agri arm, BVG Life Sciences, provides precision farming, high‑yield seeds, and extension services, increasing farm productivity by 50%. The promoter family cultivates 1,50,000 acres of irrigated land.

Park tenants benefit from BVG’s backward linkage, contract manufacturing tie‑ups, and institutional sales network.

Core Common Facilities – technical deep dive

  • 5000 MT Godown – 3 bays, fumigation, insect‑proof, pallet racking, weighbridge integration.
  • 3000 MT Cold Storage – 6 multi‑temperature chambers (-20°C, 0°C, +10°C), humidity control, backup generator.
  • Pulping + Aseptic line – 2 TPH, suitable for mango, tomato, guava. Includes de‑aerator, pasteurizer, Tetra Pak® compatible filler for bag‑in‑box (200L) and drums.
  • NABL Lab & R&D – HPLC, GC, microbiological incubators, texture analyzer. Services: nutritional labeling, shelf life studies.
  • 10 Reefer Vans – 9 MT capacity each, real‑time temperature monitoring, GPS.
  • 33/11 KV Substation – 5 MVA, HT metering, backup transformer.
  • ETP/STP/WTP – Zero liquid discharge, 100 KLD effluent treatment, 200 KLD water softening plant.
  • Pre‑ripening chambers – 384 MT, ethylene gas based, controlled atmosphere.

All facilities are available to tenants at subsidized rates under PMKSY scheme.
